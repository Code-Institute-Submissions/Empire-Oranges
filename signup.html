FAQ

Who are the family behind Empire Oranges?

The Birch family have been running Empire Farms for over 200years. It's a family run business past down through generation to generation.

What's included with my Empire Oranges subscription?

Your subsription includes between 1-9 packs of our fresh, ethical oranges each month.

How much does a subscription cost?

You can sign up and try out the subscription service for free for your first month. All for one low fixed monthly fee. Plans start from £2.99 a month. No extra costs or contracts.

How do I upgrade my subscription?

Once you sign up and create an account, you can go in to your account settings and choose to upgrade your account through the dashboard

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can easily cancel your account online in two clicks. There are no cancellation fees – start or stop your account at any time, all you have to do is go into your account settings and choose to cancel your account through the dashboard

Can I cancel my order after it's gone through?

Each month you will have the opportunity to place your order for whatever choice of oranges you prefer, and with our freindly cusomter support agents, always ready to help, you can contact them any time you need to change/ cancel an order after its gone through

What types of oranges do Empire Oranges have to offer?

Below is a detailed list fof oranges produced by Empire Farms; Valencia, Navel, Satsuma, Clementine, Pineapple oranges, Mandarin, Blood oranges, Seville, Hamlin, Jaffa, Cara Cara, Bahianinha, Moro Tarocco, Parson Brown, Pera, Bergamot, Bigaradier Apepu, Bittersweet Daidai, Gou Tou and Chinotto oranges.

Where are Empire Farms located?

Each of our farms are located in environments which are ideal for our oranges to reach their fulll potential. Our farmers follow ethical, green practices as well as our own strict guidlines to ensure a fairer trade for each of the communities involved on the surrounding site at our farms.

Can I visit an Orange Farm?

As part of our customer loyalty program, you will be able to visit one of our rustic farm cottages and enjoy a luxury break with family or friends.
